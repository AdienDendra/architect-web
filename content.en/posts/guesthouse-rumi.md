To be human is a guest house.  
Each dawn brings a new arrival.

A flash of joy, a grief, a touch of misery,  
some fleeting moment of awareness,  
appears like an unexpected guest.

Welcome and host them all!  
Even if they are a crowd of sorrows,  
who violently sweep through your house,  
emptying it until nothing remains.

Yet, treat every guest with kindness.  
They might be cleaning out your soul,  
making room for a new joy.

The dark thought, the shame, the malice,  
meet them all with a smile,  
and invite them to step inside.  

Be grateful for whoever knocks your soul,  
because each one has been sent to you,  
as a guide from beyond time.

Jalaluddin Rumi
</div>
